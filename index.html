Put Your Cloud to Sleep

Sandman automatically shuts down Azure VMs when shifts end, notifies users via Microsoft Teams, and lets them postpone with one click — so you save money without disrupting anyone.

Cut Costs on Autopilot

Scheduled shutdowns slash your cloud bill — savings while you sleep

Smart Disruption Shield

Your Cloud, Your Rules

Self-hosted on your VM — no SaaS, no third-party connectors, full control

How It Works

1

Schedule

Define shift start & end times for each VM through the admin dashboard

2

Notify

At shift end, the user gets a Teams message: “Can we stop your machine?”

3

Postpone

Need more time? Pick 1–3 hours directly from the Teams card with one click

4

Deallocate

VM shuts down automatically — savings are logged to the dashboard
